Screenshots from the experimental application

 

The Sea Surface Temperature (SST) in the South-West of Indian Ocean. On the upper left, there is the CoordinateChooser widget.

 

 

Same area than above, but showing the gradient magnitude as computed by the Sobel operator (using JAI's GradientMagnitude operation). Note that this implementation doesn't mix up land and clouds while computing gradient magnitude. Land stay land, and clouds stay clouds.

 

 

Showing a time series made of 4 images. Mediterranean coast is drawn of top of them as a vectorized layer. Geostrophic currents are displayed as small bleu arrows. A magnifier on the bottom left image is magnifying some arrows.

 

 

Showing 4 parameters in a mosaic image. Zooming in any image apply the same zoom to other images. The orange bars on the left give an overview of time range of available data.
